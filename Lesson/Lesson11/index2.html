Vietnamese tea drinking culture: past and present story

Thai Nguyen green tea has been a popular, familiar drink for generations. Tea trees appear mainly in the provinces of Thai Nguyen, Phu Tho, Yen Bai, Lam Dong… and are the source of high quality tea products.

Thai Nguyen green tea with jasmine

In Vietnam, there are many places that grow jasmine flowers. This type of flower blooms for up to 6 months a year, so natural jasmine flowers are used a lot to scent tea, and tea scented with jasmine flowers is much cheaper than tea scented with West Lake lotus flowers.

How to preserve Shan Tuyet tea to keep its aroma

Tea is the drink that loses its aroma and flavor the easiest, and also easily absorbs other strange flavors, causing the tea to gradually lose its original flavor. Preserving tea is a very important thing that every tea drinker must know.
